WELCOME TO OXEN GARAGE GALWAY

OXEN Garage Galway provides customers all over the Galway and surrounding area with the very best car servicing and car repairs available. With over 2 decades offering clients the very best car serving and repair, we’ve amassed a sterling reputation for our professionalism and fast, effective service. Our dedicated car mechanic has nearly 4 decades of experience in car repair. As such we’ve amassed the expertise and experience to quickly and effectively identify and solve any problem with your vehicle. Most of our work comes from referrals which is a testament to the quality of our work and the trust our valued customers put in our service. We deal with all makes and models and we strive for 100% customer satisfaction every time.
